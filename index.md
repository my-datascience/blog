This is my personal datascience portfolio blog. I write this with three purposes in mind:

1. To show my potential employers some of my knowledge and skills (though this blog is incomplete for this purpose).
2. For me to have sort of a library of topics that I might want to get back to later. Because it happenes to me from time to time that I need to use a concept that I know I once learned and understood, but it was some time ago and I can't remember all the details.
3. By writing this blog, I want to refresh my skills and knowledge and to kick start my datascience thinking again (see more in the upcoming post *About me*).


I'm not fully documenting my whole datascience journey here. At least now. I'm only including topics that I'm struggling with (and I learn them by writing about them) or I think are worth having here from other reasons.
